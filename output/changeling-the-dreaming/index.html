Changeling: The Dreaming

A tabletop role-playing game set in a world of faerie and dreams, exploring themes of identity and transformation.

Description

Changeling: The Dreaming is a tabletop role-playing game published by White Wolf Publishing that combines elements of fantasy, horror, and social commentary. Set in a contemporary world where the mundane and the magical intersect, players take on the roles of Changelings—humans who have been touched by the fae, resulting in a dual nature. These characters navigate the complexities of their human lives while also engaging with the realms of dreams and faerie. The game emphasizes themes of identity, transformation, and the struggle between the harsh realities of the human world and the enchanting yet perilous nature of the fae realm. Changelings often grapple with the loss of their original identity and the challenge of reconciling their human selves with their magical heritage. The game also serves as a metaphor for real-world issues such as alienation, creativity, and the pursuit of personal truth, making it a rich narrative experience that invites players to explore what it means to be 'other' in a world that often values conformity.

Examples

  • Players create characters who embody various fae archetypes, such as Sidhe, Pooka, or Beast.
  • The game features a unique system for storytelling that emphasizes personal narratives and group dynamics.

Additional Information

  • Changeling: The Dreaming is part of the World of Darkness series, which includes other games like Vampire: The Masquerade and Werewolf: The Apocalypse.
  • The game has seen several editions, with the most recent being the 20th Anniversary Edition, which revisits and expands upon the original content.
